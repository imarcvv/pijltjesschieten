# NU.nl Layout Analysis

## Key observations from the PDF screenshot:

### Header
- Dark navy/black top bar with NU.nl logo (red/white)
- Navigation: Nieuws, Sport, Tech, Economie, Entertainment, etc.
- Search icon top right

### Banner placement (RTB slots)
1. **BILLBOARD / TAKEOVER** — Top of page, full-width banner (728x90 leaderboard or 970x250 billboard)
   - The Thuisbezorgd.nl "50% korting" banner is here — large, colorful, prominent
   - This is the #1 RTB slot on NU.nl — highest visibility
2. **RIGHT RAIL (MPU)** — 300x250 or 300x600 right column
3. **IN-ARTICLE** — Between news items

### Content layout
- 2-column grid: main content left (~70%), right rail (~30%)
- News items with thumbnail images + headline + category tag
- Red category labels (Nieuws, Sport, Tech, etc.)
- Clean white/light gray background

### The Thuisbezorgd banner
- Large billboard format at top (approx 970x250 or full-width)
- Yellow/orange background
- "50% korting" in large bold text
- Food imagery (burger/fries)
- This is a classic RTB billboard placement

### Dart flyover concept
- Darts enter from LEFT side of screen
- Fly OVER the banner (z-index above the ad)
- Can also fly through the news content area
- Homepage takeover: dart flies from left edge to right edge
- On click: dart "lands" and unfolds to reveal sponsor message
